How to launch the serverpack

1 : open start.bat(right click ,edit) or start.sh with a text oppener (.sh for linux) (.bat for windows)

2 : change the default JAVA_HOME with your java home (don't forget to use java.exe(for windows) or java(for linux) at the end of the JAVA_HOME)

3(windows) : double left click on the start.bat

3(linux) : right click in the root folder of the serverpack, Open terminal here and use "./start.sh" command without ""
